MusicMap

MusicMap is a versatile web application allowing users to search for musicians and visualize both past and upcoming shows on an interactive map. The platform provides detailed information about concert venues, dates, and locations, facilitating efficient concert planning and retrospective exploration of the live music scene. Developed with a focus on practicality and information accuracy, MusicMap serves as a versatile tool for users to navigate through both historical and upcoming concerts.
